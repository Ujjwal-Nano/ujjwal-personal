Some feelings just stay quietly in your heart for a long time… until one day, you can’t really keep them to yourself anymore. I’ve felt this for a while. I wasn’t always sure, but now I am. I don’t want to regret not saying it.

The moment I noticed you were unlike anyone else

I didn’t expect things to go this way: from Hinge to Instagram to WhatsApp, and then actually meeting you. But somewhere along the way, you became someone I genuinely look forward to talking to.

The more I got to know you, whether in person or through chats, the more I started noticing the little things: the way you think, the way you carry yourself, the way you see the world. It just felt different in a way I couldn’t ignore.

I’ve always admired people who are self-aware, confident, and focused on growing personally and professionally. And honestly, the more I see you, the more I realize you fit into all of that so effortlessly.

There’s a lot to learn from you. Sure, we think a little differently sometimes, but that’s what makes it interesting. Like we both agree on one thing — “In heaven, there’s no beer, that’s why we drink it here.”

The day I don’t talk to you, something feels missing in my day. I know it might not be the same for you, but it definitely is for me.

And yeah, I’m not going to lie, you being a die-hard RCB fan hurts a bit. But I can manage. After all, I still have a soft spot for a few of their former players and a few current ones too.

Look, I’m writing all this from my perspective, based on my feelings. You might have a completely different perspective on things, and that’s okay. I just needed to get this off my chest. I’ve been holding it in for a while and would’ve regretted not telling you at some point.

Please feel free to share your thoughts on all this. I’m open to hearing whatever you have to say, whether it’s about how you see our connection or anything else.

What you do to my heart

Normally, I’m pretty confident when talking to people, but it’s weird: when I talk to you, I get this feeling of being slightly more conscious of what I say. It’s like I want to say the right thing, I guess that's just the effect you have on me.

You mean something to me, Deepika.
